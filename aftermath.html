The revolution did not end with the fall of the emperor. Under the Derg, life changed quickly. New rules were put in place. Farms were taken over. Schools were reorganized. But along with reform came violence. The period known as the Red Terror began. People were arrested without trial. Families lost loved ones. Fear spread through towns and villages.

Some left the country. Others stayed and hoped for better days. Life was harder for many, especially in rural areas. Food shortages continued. Jobs were scarce. Still, people adapted. They found ways to survive in a country that felt unfamiliar.
